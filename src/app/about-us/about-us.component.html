Who We Are?

Pragra is a leading technology company leveraging the power of AI and digital era to automate your product development and test automation. We assist our customer to optimize their process to maximize their profits/savings.

We strive to be thought leaders, not industry followers and always stay abreast of the latest products, methods, and technology so you don’t have to. We nourished in our People and their capability and our framework to support the achievement of high performance, both from an individual and organizational perspective.

We have a proven track record of delivery and our focus is to partner with our clients to understand their testing needs and help them put in place the right resourcing, bootcamp or service solutions to meet their every unique requirement.

Our Approach

Pragra’s aim is to continue to grow as the best provider of independent testing and assurance services with an emphasis on building a trust and being the place of choice for test and assurance professionals to work.

We engage early to get the truest view we listen to every one. Our fieldwork is non-judgmental and an opportunity for all participants to confidentially share their honest perspectives. Every observation and recommendation is carefully considered and backed by evidence.

Our Bootcamps

IT industry is changing at a rapid pace and you need today’s skill to be a successful professional. Your business need these skills to make use of latest technologies to deliver seamless services to end consumer.

At Pragra, we have invested in our consultant to deliver the skills required in today’s enterprise. Our consultants have delivered thousands of hours corporate and individual bootcamp/program and shaped the career of hundreds of professionals.

Pragra follows the unique blend of methodology and assignment based program so you get more out of it. Our programs are designed to help learners develop the skills and professional network needed to capitalize on the demand for dynamic people required in industry.
